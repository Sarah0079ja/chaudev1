The Process

Three steps. Zero effort from you.

From new lead to booked appointment without you lifting a finger.

New lead comes in: agent texts them immediately

The moment a lead submits a form, calls, or comes in from Zillow or Realtor.com, your AI agent fires off a personalized SMS within 15 seconds. Every other agent is asleep or with a client. You're already in the lead's phone.

Agent asks 3 qualifying questions via SMS

Budget range. Timeline to buy or sell. Pre-approval status. The agent holds a natural back-and-forth SMS conversation (not a robotic form) and gathers exactly what you need to know before the meeting.

Qualified lead gets booked into your calendar

Once qualified, the agent offers your real availability and books the appointment directly into your calendar. You get a notification with the full conversation log. You show up to a warm, prepared meeting.
